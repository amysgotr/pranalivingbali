The morning ritual

01
Open a window
Let the morning air find you before anything else does. Before the phone. Before the plans. Just air.
02
Set one intention
Not a list. One word. One feeling. One thing you want to carry into the day. That's enough.
03
Light your Sandalwood
Hold the stick gently. As the smoke lifts, let it take your intention with it, out into the world.
04
Breathe in the clarity
In for 4. Hold briefly. Out for 6. The bright scent opens. The exhale releases. Do this three times.
05
Begin
You are ready. Not because everything is figured out. Because you chose to begin anyway. That is enough.
