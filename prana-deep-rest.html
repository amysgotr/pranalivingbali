The ritual

01
Dim your space
Let the room soften. Screens off. This hour is yours now, not anyone else's.
02
Light with intention
Before you strike the match, hold one thought: I am releasing today. Then let it go.
03
Breathe 4 · 4 · 8
In for 4. Hold for 4. Out slowly for 8. Let the warm wood scent do half the work.
04
Speak your mantra
Quietly, or only in your mind. Let the words land in your body like smoke fills a room. No effort. Just presence.
05
Surrender
Nothing more is needed. The stick burns for 3 hours. The scent stays 7. You don't have to hold on to anything tonight.
